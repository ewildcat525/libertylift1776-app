# "Reps for the Republic" Tee — Screen Print Specs

Production-ready art for the LibertyLift1776 "Reps for the Republic" shirt.
All art is **vector** (SVG + PDF) at final print size, plus 300 DPI transparent
PNGs for shops that prefer raster. Two print locations, two spot colors each.

## Ink colors (both locations)

| Plate | Color | Pantone | Hex reference |
|-------|-------|---------|---------------|
| 1 | Black | Black C (or shop standard black) | `#000000` |
| 2 | Red | **PMS 193 C** (Old Glory Red) | `#B22234` |

- Colors are solid spot inks at 100% — **no halftones, no gradients**.
- The plates do not overlap; no trapping required.
- On **white garments**: no underbase needed.
- On colored garments: printer to add a standard white underbase under both plates.
- Distressed/grunge texture is **intentional** and is built into the vector paths — do not "clean it up."

## Location 1 — Full front

- **Art size: 10.98" wide x 16.0" tall** (files include a small transparent margin; document is 11.56" x 16.58")
- Placement: centered, top of art approx. 3" below the collar seam.
- If a smaller print is preferred, scale proportionally (e.g. 9.6" x 14"); art is vector and scales cleanly.
- Files:
  - `front-print.svg` / `front-print.pdf` — combined 2-color art (plates on separate layers/groups: `plate-black`, `plate-red-PMS193C`)
  - `front-sep-black.svg` / `.pdf` — black plate only
  - `front-sep-red.svg` / `.pdf` — red plate only
  - `front-print-300dpi.png` — 300 DPI raster, transparent background

## Location 2 — Back vertical spine

- **Art size: 2.02" wide x 16.0" tall** (document is 2.58" x 16.55" with margin)
- Placement: centered on spine, top star approx. 2" below the collar seam.
- The red stripes fade out with a distressed drip at the bottom — this is part of the design.
- Files:
  - `back-print.svg` / `back-print.pdf` — combined 2-color art (same layer structure)
  - `back-sep-black.svg` / `.pdf` — black plate only
  - `back-sep-red.svg` / `.pdf` — red plate only
  - `back-print-300dpi.png` — 300 DPI raster, transparent background

## Suggested production notes (printer's discretion)

- Mesh: 156–200 for the front (fine distressed detail), 156 for the back stripes.
- Front black plate carries fine texture in the lettering and flag — avoid over-flooding to keep the grunge open.

## Provenance

Art was extracted and vectorized from the original design mockup
(`mockup-original.png`). The combined SVGs are the source of truth; the PDFs
and PNGs are exported from them.
